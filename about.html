I am a usability enthusiast and an Internet of Things visionary. I am passionate about testing the usability of software and making workable recommendations for fixing the issues found, in addition to conceptualizing and designing usable software myself. I believe in the mountain-moving power of teamwork and I value the contributions of my teammates. I am always ready to roll up my sleeves and write serious code if I have to. However, I believe in doing one thing really really well and that one thing for me is user-centric design. Making the world an exciting place that is welcoming to all users irrespective of their physical and mental abilities is the driving force behind my existence as a designer.
